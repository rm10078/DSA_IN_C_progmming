Bubble sort is a sorting algorithm that compares two adjacent elements and 
swaps them util they are not in the intended order.

data[] is array, si is size of array.

1.reapeat j=0 to j<si-1
    reapeat i=0 to i<si-j-1
        if(data[i]>data[i+1])
            swap data[i] with data[i+1]
        end if
2.stop
